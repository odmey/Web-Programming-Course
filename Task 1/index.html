KOZ Entertainment

BOYNEXTDOOR (보이넥스트도어) is a 6-member South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment consisting of Jaehyun, Sungho, Riwoo, Taesan, Leehan, and Woonhak. They debuted on May 30, 2023 with their first single album WHO!. They made their Japanese debut on July 10, 2024.
